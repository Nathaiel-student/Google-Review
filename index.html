Google Review Process

Step 1

Ensure your Gmail meets these requirements: GMAIL REQUIREMENTS:
✅ Verified by phone number
✅ Active for a year or more
✅ Email and name should match (e.g., akkimae456@gmail.com should be Akki Mae)
✅ Preferably a personal email to avoid flagged reviews
✅ Birth year on Gmail account should be 2000 or earlier (you can change this in your Google account settings)
